> Supoose i am opening 5 dialouge box i a widget now whats happening is
> that the dialouge box opened last remains on top of the other dialouge
> box opened previously. I want to move that dialouge box on top to which
> my mouse currently points. Is there any way to do it .. please help..

You can use the z-index property to control the stack order of
positioned elements.  You must ensure that the active dialog has the
largest z-index value.  Try:

DOM.setStyleAttribute(yourElement, "zIndex", 1000);
